About me

Hi, my name is Anna and about a year ago I decided to start my journey on becoming front-end developer. My first encounter with programming was back in the high school and I enjoyed making simple pages in HTML and CSS but it was just one of many hobbies. Few years later when I was on vocational course for IT Technician my interest in web development was reignited and I thought that it was something I want to pursue as a future career. I completed few courses on Udemy platform and took 9-month Web Developer Bootcamp by Kodilla. Right now I'm focusing on improving my skills by making small projects and complement my knowledge with online resources. What I love about programming is the opportunity of creating something that can be used and experienced by others and the chance to learn how to solve problems, by digging out why something doesn't work the way it should.
